About

I am a software developer working on machine learning at SAP. I received a Ph.D. in Theoretical Physics from Indiana University Bloomington with minors in nuclear physics and high-energy physics. After two years working as a postdoctoral researcher at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology in Germany, I made the transition to industry. Now I enjoy a daily dose of Python coding, mining text data, machine learning, and playing with maps. My fascination for physics is now streamed via Twitter and a blog for the general public.
