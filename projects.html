Physics of explosions

Physics of the Beirut explosion

On August 4, 2020 a devastating explosion occurred at the port of Lebanonâ€™s capital Beirut. Using
videos from social media and basic image processing, I studied the growth of the fireball as a function
of time to estimate the explosive yield of the explosion. Using the Sedov-Taylor-von Neumann relation
for the early stage of a blast wave, the data imply the release of 2.3 TJ, equivalent over half a kiloton
of TNT.

The theory, methodology, and analysis were published as Explosion analysis from images:
Trinity and Beirut, Eur. J. Phys. 42, 035803 (2021) doi:10.1088/1361-6404/abe131, available
at arXiv:2009.05674
